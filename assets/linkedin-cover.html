Export to PNG for LinkedIn

The frame above is exactly 1200 x 627 (LinkedIn's recommended share image size).

Three ways to convert:

  1. Open this file in Chrome or Safari, right-click the image, "Save image as..." (saves SVG). Then use an online converter (cloudconvert.com, svgomg) to get PNG at 1200x627.
  2. In macOS: open the SVG in Preview or Pixelmator, export as PNG at 1200x627.
  3. Use bash: rsvg-convert -w 1200 -h 627 linkedin-cover.svg -o linkedin-cover.png (requires brew install librsvg).
